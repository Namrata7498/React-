Selling Essentials Program
Being a successful seller requires basic skills. If you are a sales professional seeking to improve the effectiveness of your ground level sales techniques, enroll in the Selling Essentials Program. This program is targeted to those who are new to the sales role or even intermediate level sellers who may want to brush up on basic skills.
Selling Essentials Program
Learning Journey topics include
  • Sales Foundation concepts (prospecting, diagnosing, presenting, overcoming objections, negotiating,and closing)
  • General Listening
  • General Communication
  • General presentation delivery
  • General finance concepts for non-finance professionals

You will also choose an elective learning journey to complete the program. Choose from options such as leadership, time management, advanced selling essentials, applying Generative AI in the sales process, and even social selling. The training content includes online, self-paced video courses and assessments provided via Skillsoft’s Percipio education platform. There is no cost associated with registration. The program will run for 12 weeks, from April 8 - June 28, with a total of a 12 hour time commitment. Thats just 1 hour a week.

Registration is open from March 11 – 29, 2024.

Audience
Those who are new to selling or who may want to brush up on the essential and fundamental skills including selling, listening and communication, general presentation delivery, and basic finance concepts for non-finance professionals.
Completion Requirements
Complete 5 Required Learning Journeys + 1 Elective Learning Journey of your choice.
Award
Earn a digital badge for every course your complete. Receive a Selling Essentials Program Certificate of Completion when all program requirements are met.
Your Success
Participants should leave the program with basic exposure to the learning journey topics provided to help facilitate a positive foundation for knowing how to interact with potential buyers on a regular basis.
